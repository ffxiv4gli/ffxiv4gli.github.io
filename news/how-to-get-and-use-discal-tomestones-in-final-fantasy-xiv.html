How to Get and Use Discal Tomestones in Final Fantasy XIV?

FFXIV4Gil Date: Mar/08/22 15:17:10 Views: 467

Final Fantasy XIV comes with tons of items and equipment, Discal Tomestone is one of the items you get inside the game. But what are Discal Tomestones, how do you get and use them? Here's what you need to know about how to get and use Discal Tomestones in Final Fantasy XIV.

What is Discal Tomestone in Final Fantasy XIV?

Discal Tomestones are the currency used to obtain the brand new Radiant Weapon items.

How to Get Discal Tomestones in Final Fantasy XIV?

Discal Tomestone is not hard to get, but it is time-consuming. Here's what you need to know about how to get Discal Tomestones in Final Fantasy XIV:

1). Discal Tomestones are an end game material, which means that you'll first need to have completed the Endwalker MSQ and have your character at level 90 with a combat class.

2). You can then now begin the Pandæmonium: Asphodelos raid.

3). You'll have to defeat the Asphodelos: The Fourth Circle raid that the fourth and final raid in the Pandaemonium: Asphodelos raid series.

4). Once you do, you will begin earning the 'Unsung Blade of Asphodelos'.

5). You need to earn a total of seven 'Unsung blades' to earn one Discal Tomestone. It should be noted that only one 'Unsung Blade of Asphodelos' can be earned per week and hence, it will take some time to gather a lot of Discal Tomestones.

6). Once you've acquired seven Unsung Blades of Asphodelos, you can head to one of two particular vendors to make the trade.

7). You will be able to find one vendor named Mylenie in Labyrinthos at the exact coordinates X:8.4, Y:27.6.

8). Or you can find Djole in Radz-at-Han at the exact coordinates once again of X:10.3, Y:9.6.

9). Both of these vendors will likely have the same items for sale and trading with your character. You'll be able to turn in your Blades for one Discal Tomestone.

How to Use Discal Tomestones in Final Fantasy XIV?

Discal Tomestones are used to obtain even more powerful weapons for each individual class in FFXIV. Once you have one Tomestone, you can speak to Djole again to using the Discal Tomestones to offer them for weapons. You can acquire a Radiant Weapon using a Discal Tomestone and 500 of the new Allagan Tomestone of Astronomy. Alternatively, if you're a Paladin, you'll only need 350 Allagan Tomestone of Astronomy and a single Discal Tomestone.

It's worth noting that if you want to level up multiple classes, you'll need to farm the 'Unsung Blades of Asphodelos' again to get another Discal Tomestones.
